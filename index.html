About

I am a Graduate student at American University specializing in the ethical and social implications of Disability, Gender, Race, and Technology.

Research

My research focuses on the intersection of disability studies, gender, race, and technology. I examine how these fields intersect to shape experiences of inclusion and exclusion in health care, education, and in digital spaces.
