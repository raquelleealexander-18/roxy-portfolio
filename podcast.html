About the Podcast

Philosophy Across Generations brings philosophical conversations into community spaces, breaking down the walls of the Ivory Tower. As co-host, I partner with DC's Global Kids and the American Red Cross to create programming that makes philosophy accessible and relevant to people of all ages.

The podcast explores how philosophical ideas shape our everyday lives, examining questions of ethics, justice, identity, and meaning through intergenerational dialogue.

Community Partners

  • DC's Global Kids — Youth-focused community programming
  • American Red Cross — Community outreach and engagement
  • AU Humanities Truck — Bringing philosophy to public spaces
